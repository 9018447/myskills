## Major Publishers

**Wiley Online Library**
- https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/
- Portal for all Wiley journals

**Elsevier / ScienceDirect**
- https://www.sciencedirect.com/
- Large collection of STEM journals

**Springer**
- https://link.springer.com/
- Springer Nature journals

**ACS Publications**
- https://pubs.acs.org/
- American Chemical Society journals

**IEEE Xplore**
- https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/
- Engineering and computer science

## Preprint Servers

**arXiv**
- https://arxiv.org/
- API: https://export.arxiv.org/api/query?
- Categories: physics, math, CS, quantitative biology, etc.

**bioRxiv**
- https://www.biorxiv.org/
- Biology preprints

**chemRxiv**
- https://chemrxiv.org/
- Chemistry preprints

## Search Engines

**Google Scholar**
- https://scholar.google.com/
- General academic search
- Shows "PDF" links to institutional copies

**PubMed**
- https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/
- Biomedical literature
- API available

## Access Tools

**Unpaywall**
- Browser extension
- Finds legal open-access versions
- https://unpaywall.org/

## Notes
- Most paywalled sites require institutional VPN/network access
- Direct scraping often blocked by Cloudflare or bot detection
- APIs (arXiv, PubMed) are more reliable than scraping HTML
